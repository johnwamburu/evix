Frequently Asked Questions

This is a short list of our most frequently asked questions. For more info about VX, or if you need support, please contact us.

What exactly is VX ISM?

VX-ISM (Virtual Exchange – Integrated School Manager) is a state of the art school information management system developed using the latest Python development & test tools whose main priority is to enhance the efficiency in which school related tasks, both academic and administrative, are performed. VX-ISM incorporates various modules, some of which are interdependent, into one complete package. These inbuilt modules are tasked with the responsibility of handling core school management activities ranging from students admission and examination analysis to the financial management of cash flows.

There's talk all over about VX SaaS - can you tell me more?

Vx Saas (Software as a Service) is an online platform of the Vx ISM which is hosted by our central Vx servers. This enables easy access of the system, by the school’s administrative body, from any location and at their convenience through a reliable internet connection. Introduction of Vx SaaS aims at enhancing the flexibility of school administration, while still maintaining utmost efficiency levels.

I want to acquire VX Express C - where do I start?

Vx Express C is a fully customizable version of the standard Vx ISM. It is simply a tailor made version of Vx ISM developed under the principles of dynamism and professionalism, in the sense that client specifications/additional tweaks are incorporated into the system to address diverse client needs and satisfaction. Vx Express C is an “on request” service/product, subject to discussions with the client. To leave an order please navigate to “PRODUCTS” from the main menu and click on Vx Express C.

How is VX different from other systems?

Vx ISM consists of various modules that handle virtually all the school related tasks ranging from the academic to administrative aspects. These modules ease school management with regards to the following fields: Examination analysis and reports, school financial management, student discipline and monitoring, library and stock taking, internal and external communication (SMS alerts), and last but not least, student database and human resource management. In addition to that, Vx ISM offers a comprehensive and detailed analysis of all these aspects. With such components in place, school management efficiency levels can only get better.

What exactly is VX School-Pages?

The Vx School Pages is an integrated fully fledged interactive SaaS (Software as aService) platform that incorporates a fully functional school website with a perpetual online portal. The online portal creates a virtual link between the school and the relevant users whereby concerned parents can easily access past and present overall school performances and keep close tabs on their sons/daughters in terms of their performance, class attendance and overall discipline. This module also accommodates an alumni community with an efficient communication channel for the purposes of posting important school news from the school’s administrative body.
